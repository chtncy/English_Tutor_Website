FAQ

What’s included in the Basic plan?
The Basic plan includes up to 5 one‑on‑one lessons per month, email support, and full access to our resource library.
Can I change my plan later?
Absolutely—you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.
Do you offer group lessons?
We currently specialize in individual tutoring, but group sessions may be added in the future—stay tuned!
