About Me

Language Leap was founded by myself, a CELTA certified English tutor from London, England. With a degree in Psychology and a passion for helping others grow, I've got years of experience (UK and UAE) teaching hundreds of students across all ages and levels. My personalised and interactive approach aims to create a supportive space where students feel confident and motivated to improve their English skills.

Services

General English

My General English classes are designed to help you improve all areas of the language — reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Each lesson is tailored to your level and learning goals, with practical topics, clear explanations, and fun activities to help you grow in confidence and ability.

  • Lessons based on the Evolve series by Cambridge
  • Practice in grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation
  • Interactive slides, listening tasks & games
  • Extra support with worksheets and quizzes
  • Personal feedback to help you grow
  • Private Conversation Practice

Speaking

Want to speak more fluently and feel confident in real conversations? These one-to-one speaking sessions focus on improving your conversation skills, pronunciation, and natural flow. We’ll talk about everyday topics or themes that matter to you — all in a relaxed, supportive environment.

  • Speaking-focused sessions to boost your confidence
  • Real-world topics and useful phrases
  • Instant feedback and correction
  • Tips to sound more natural and fluent
  • Support between lessons if you need help

FAQ

What types of classes do you offer and what platform do you use?
I offer online classes on Google Meet.
Do you teach all levels of English?
Yes! I work with learners from beginner to advanced levels, tailoring lessons to suit your goals and needs.
How do payments and bookings work?
All classes must be paid in advance via the payment link on the website. You can choose hourly sessions or discounted packages. After buying a lesson or package, you can book your classes using the online calendar. For hourly lessons, you book each class one by one. For packages, you also book each lesson one at a time until all lessons are used. The system tracks how many lessons you have left.
How long are lesson packages valid?
10-lesson packages are valid for 3 months, and 20-lesson packages are valid for 6 months from purchase. Please complete your lessons within this time. Unused lessons after expiration cannot be refunded.
Can I cancel or reschedule a class?
Yes, with at least 24 hours' notice. Late cancellations or missed sessions are non-refundable.
What materials do you use?
I use trusted resources like Evolve by Cambridge, along with custom materials designed to keep lessons engaging and practical.
